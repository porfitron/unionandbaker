Investment Opportunity

Ideally situated in Cow Hollow, bordering Pacific Heights and the Presidio Edge.

Prominently sitting on the corner of Baker and Union Streets, this trophy building has been extensively renovated. The location offers unmatched walkability to iconic San Francisco landmarks including the Lyon Street Steps, Palace of Fine Arts, Lucas Arts Letterman Campus, and East Beach.

Total Building Configuration

The property consists of two distinct units comprising a total of 5 bedrooms, 5 bathrooms, 2 full kitchens, and 2 laundry rooms. Parking is exceptional for the area, featuring 3 individual garage spaces, each with its own separate door.

Unit Breakdown

  • Unit 1 (Upper Penthouse): A stunning 2-level, 3-bedroom, 3-bath flat. This Penthouse features a walk-out deck offering breathtaking views of the San Francisco Bay, Angel Island, Alcatraz, Tiburon, the Marina, and Billionaires Row.
  • Unit 2 (Lower): A full-floor 2-bedroom, 2-bath flat featuring a great room and plenty of San Francisco charm and modern luxury.

Extensive Modern Upgrades

Significant capital improvements have been made to modernize the building's efficiency, security, and aesthetics:

  • Exterior: New Roof (2021) and completely re-surfaced exterior stucco.
  • Climate Control: New vented HVAC heatpump system for heating and cooling.
  • Efficiency: New heatpump water heaters installed.
  • Windows & Treatments: New double-paned windows throughout, fitted with custom shades. The top floor features electric smart shades that operate on command or programmable schedules.
  • Security: New security gate, keyless entry with smart locks, doorbell, security cameras, and updated exterior lighting.
  • Ambiance: Electric fireplace inserts with remote controls installed in each unit.

ADU Opportunity

The largest opportunity for further customization is the ADU/bonus room. This space already features fittings for its own full bathroom and installed composite wood-style flooring. Completing this space would bring the total building count to 6 Bedrooms and 6 Bathrooms.
